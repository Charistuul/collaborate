Collaborate "Trade for Photo"

Photography is a passion of mine and I do this because I love to create and I enjoy working with others who share this passion. Our shoot will be energetic and fun! We will talk out ideas and through your vision and mine we will develope a unique theme!

    What to expect from me, your Photographer

  • As your photographer, I will provide professional guidance in posing and wardrobe as we bring our ideas to fruition! I will listen to you as we work and our shoot will result in magazine worthy images that you will be proud to share!

  • I provide professional level work resulting from decades of experience and look forward to working with models who want to take their work to the next level!

    • What I expect from you, the Model

  • As a model please be ready to discuss ideas during the consultation. I want your input! If you are new to modeling lets get some images that will look amazing in your portfolio! Show up ready to work on shoot day, bring the essentials (brush, makeup and any special accessories) along with any wardrobe of your own that you may wish to use. I do provide some wardrobe and it will be very helpful to have your sizing information in the planning stages.
